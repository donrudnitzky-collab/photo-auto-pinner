Privacy Policy — Photo Auto-Pinner
Last updated: July 14, 2026

Photo Auto-Pinner is a personal automation tool that publishes photos from the owner's own collection to the owner's own Pinterest boards.
Data we collect
None. This application:

is used exclusively by its owner on the owner's own Pinterest account;
does not have any third-party users;
does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data;
does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
How the Pinterest API is used
The app uses the Pinterest API only to create pins (title, description, image, link) on boards belonging to the account owner, and to read the list of the owner's boards. Access tokens are stored privately by the owner and are never shared with anyone.
Third-party services
Images may be processed by the Anthropic API solely to generate pin titles and descriptions. No personal data is included in these requests.
